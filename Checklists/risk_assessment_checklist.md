# Risk Assessment Checklist

> Use this checklist to perform periodic risk assessments.

---

## 1. Preparation
- [ ] System boundary defined  
- [ ] Assets identified  
- [ ] Stakeholders engaged  

---

## 2. Threat Identification
- [ ] Threats identified for all major components  
- [ ] Insider threat considered  
- [ ] External threat actors identified  

---

## 3. Vulnerability Analysis
- [ ] Known vulnerabilities documented  
- [ ] Configuration weaknesses reviewed  
- [ ] Software inventory checked  

---

## 4. Likelihood & Impact
- [ ] Likelihood assigned per threat scenario  
- [ ] Impact assessed  
- [ ] Risk level calculated  

---

## 5. Recommendations
- [ ] Controls recommended  
- [ ] Risks categorized (High/Medium/Low)  
- [ ] Residual risk documented  

---

## 6. Reporting
- [ ] RA report created  
- [ ] Risk register updated  
- [ ] Management review completed
