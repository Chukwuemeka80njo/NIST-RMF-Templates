# Evidence Collection Checklist

> Use this checklist to collect and maintain audit-ready security evidence.

---

## 1. Policy & Procedure Evidence

- ☐ Security policies updated annually  
- ☐ Procedures reviewed and approved  
- ☐ Version control documented  
- ☐ Signed approval records available  

---

## 2. Technical Evidence

- ☐ System configuration screenshots  
- ☐ Access control lists  
- ☐ Audit logs (30–90 days retention)  
- ☐ Patch records  
- ☐ Scan results  

---

## 3. Administrative Evidence

- ☐ Training records  
- ☐ Incident response reports  
- ☐ Background check documentation  
- ☐ Contractor security agreements  

---

## 4. Control-Specific Evidence

- **AC (Access Control)**  
  - ☐ User access reviews  
  - ☐ MFA enforcement evidence  
  - ☐ Account management documentation  

- **CM (Configuration Management)**  
  - ☐ Approved baseline configurations  
  - ☐ Change requests  

- **SI (System Integrity)**  
  - ☐ Antivirus logs  
  - ☐ Monitoring alerts  
  - ☐ Patching records  

---

## 5. Storage & Protection of Evidence

- ☐ Evidence stored in secure repository  
- ☐ Access restricted to authorized staff  
- ☐ Backup copies maintained  
- ☐ Evidence retention schedule followed  

---

## 6. Audit Preparation

- ☐ Evidence linked to corresponding controls  
- ☐ Audit folder complete  
- ☐ Sensitive information sanitized  
- ☐ Evidence validated for accuracy  

---
