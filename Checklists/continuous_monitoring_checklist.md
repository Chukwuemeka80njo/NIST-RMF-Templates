# Continuous Monitoring Checklist

> Use this checklist to track ongoing monitoring activities for your system.

---

## 1. Vulnerability Scanning

- ☐ Weekly authenticated scans performed  
- ☐ Monthly external scans performed  
- ☐ Findings reviewed and prioritized  
- ☐ High-risk vulnerabilities remediated within required timeframe  
- ☐ Scan results archived for audit  

---

## 2. Patch & Update Management

- ☐ OS patches applied within organizational timelines  
- ☐ Application updates reviewed and applied  
- ☐ Emergency patches handled appropriately  
- ☐ Patch failures documented  

---

## 3. Log Management & Review

- ☐ Security logs collected from all key systems  
- ☐ Logs aggregated (SIEM or central log server)  
- ☐ Daily log review performed  
- ☐ Suspicious activity escalated  
- ☐ Log retention meets policy  

---

## 4. Account & Access Review

- ☐ Monthly privileged account review  
- ☐ Quarterly user account recertification  
- ☐ Immediate removal of terminated accounts  
- ☐ MFA enforced for all admin accounts  

---

## 5. Configuration Monitoring

- ☐ Baseline configurations documented  
- ☐ Changes tracked and logged  
- ☐ Unauthorized changes identified  
- ☐ Configuration deviations remediated  

---

## 6. Incident Monitoring

- ☐ Alerts configured for critical events  
- ☐ Incident handling procedures followed  
- ☐ Post-incident analysis documented  
- ☐ Indicators of compromise reviewed  

---

## 7. Reporting

- ☐ Weekly monitoring status documented  
- ☐ Monthly security dashboard updated  
- ☐ Quarterly POA&M updates completed  

---
