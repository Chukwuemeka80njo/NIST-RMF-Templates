# Continuous Monitoring Strategy

**File:** continuous_monitoring_strategy.md  
**Category:** RMF Artifacts  
**Standard:** NIST SP 800-137

---

## 1. Purpose
Defines the structured approach for ongoing monitoring of security controls to maintain situational awareness and risk posture.

---

## 2. Monitoring Components
- Control effectiveness tracking  
- Vulnerability scanning  
- Configuration management  
- Patch management  
- Log review and SIEM alerts  
- Incident reporting  
- Asset inventory updates  

---

## 3. Monitoring Frequencies
| Control Type | Frequency |
|---------------|-----------|
| High-risk controls | Monthly |
| Moderate-risk controls | Quarterly |
| Low-risk controls | Annually |

---

## 4. Metrics & Reporting
- Number of vulnerabilities by severity  
- Mean time to detect/respond (MTTD/MTTR)  
- Patch compliance percentage  
- Control deficiency trends

---

## 5. Roles & Responsibilities
- System Owner  
- ISSO  
- SOC Analysts  
- Change Manager

---

## 6. Approval
Signatures of responsible parties.
